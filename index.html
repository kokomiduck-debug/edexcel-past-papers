💡 File Naming Tip:

For beautiful auto-generated paper titles, name your PDFs in the Edexcel format:
[SubjectCode]_[Paper]_[DocType]_[Date].pdf
E.g., 4CH1_1C_que_20230518.pdf will appear as:
Paper 1C - Question Paper (June 2023)

  • que / qp = Question Paper
  • msc / ms = Mark Scheme
  • rms = Regional Mark Scheme
  • er = Examiner Report
